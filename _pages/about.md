I am now a fourth-year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. [Xin Zhang](https://xinpl.github.io) at [Programming Languages Lab](https://pl.cs.pku.edu.cn/en/), [Software Engineering Institute](https://www.sei.pku.edu.cn), [School of Computer Science](https://cs.pku.edu.cn/English/Home.htm), [Peking University](https://english.pku.edu.cn). My research focuses mainly on a new paradigm of program analysis that incorporates Bayesian reasoning. My research focuses on program analysis, especially new paradigms that incorporate Bayesian reasoning. I am also interested in combining program analysis with large language models(LLMs). Currently, I am developing a new theory which is a probabilistic extension to the famous abstract interpretation framework, which provide a theory guarantee for our Bayesian program analysis.
